Lesson Logger Companion

Fast. Focused. In-the-moment lesson capture. Built to work seamlessly with ForeThought.

Why a companion app exists

ForeThought is intentionally designed for structured reflection on macOS. It is not built for live input during a coaching session.

Lessons move quickly. Thoughts change. Drills evolve. Lesson Logger Companion exists to capture everything as it happens — without compromising ForeThought’s philosophy.

Structured lesson capture

Lesson Logger Companion is designed specifically for coaching sessions. It keeps the interface minimal and responsive while capturing meaningful data.

  • Lesson date and coach tracking
  • Session focus (e.g. driver, wedges, putting)
  • Key swing changes and feels
  • Drills assigned
  • Practice priorities
  • Ball flight notes and tendencies
  • Structured session notes
  • Quick-edit workflow during live sessions

Focused by design

There are no dashboards. No analytics. No exports.

Lesson Logger Companion does one job well: capture information clearly and efficiently while you are on the range.

Integrated workflow

Lessons are exported as structured JSON files and imported directly into ForeThought.

Once imported, lessons live alongside rounds, practice sessions, equipment data, and performance trends — creating a complete, long-term development record.

Your data. Your control.

There is no account system, no cloud sync, and no hosted service. Lessons are stored as plain structured data and transferred manually.

You decide where your data lives.

Lesson Logger Companion and ForeThought form a two-part system: capture in the moment, reflect with clarity.
