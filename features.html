ForeThought

Features

ForeThought is a macOS-native golf performance journal designed for retrospective logging. It focuses on clarity, context, and long-term improvement — not live shot capture.

Dashboard & activity timeline

The Home view provides a chronological timeline of all logged activity — rounds, practice sessions, lessons, and fitness.

Each entry is designed to be readable at a glance, while still linking to deeper detail when required.

Round & competition logging

Log rounds after play with full context: course, ball, bag, notes, and scoring. ForeThought prioritises reflective input rather than in-play interaction.

Rounds act as the foundation for long-term performance review and PDF reporting.

Practice session logging

Practice sessions are logged retrospectively and linked directly to the clubs used. This allows meaningful review without disrupting time on the range.

Sessions support notes, focus areas, and session intent — encouraging deliberate practice rather than raw volume.

Equipment management

ForeThought includes full equipment tracking, including:

  • Multiple bags
  • Club inventory
  • Club ordering by number (not loft)

Equipment data feeds directly into practice analysis and reporting.

Wedge matrix & gapping tables

The wedge matrix and gapping tables provide a structured view of yardages across the bag. These are designed to support real-world decision making rather than theoretical loft charts.

Gapping is displayed in a clean, readable format suitable for both self-review and export.

Ball & course management

Balls and courses are managed as first-class data objects.

This allows rounds and practice sessions to be viewed in context — accounting for conditions, ball choice, and venue.

Fitness & non-golf activity tracking

ForeThought supports fitness and non-golf performance logging.

This allows physical preparation and conditioning to sit alongside golf activity, recognising that performance is not built on practice alone.

Coach-ready PDF exports

ForeThought generates clean, monochrome PDF reports suitable for sharing with a coach.

Exports focus on clarity and structure rather than decoration, ensuring the data remains the priority.

Lesson Logger Companion integration

Lessons can be logged via the Lesson Logger Companion app and imported into ForeThought via JSON.

Lessons ultimately live inside ForeThought, where they can be reviewed alongside practice, rounds, and reports.

ForeThought is actively developed and evolving. Feature scope reflects the current v1.9.x build.
