ForeThought

Availability

ForeThought is currently a private macOS application under active development.

Current status

ForeThought is in regular personal use and is actively evolving. Features, workflows, and exports are refined through real-world logging rather than public release cycles.

Platform

ForeThought is built exclusively for macOS.

It is designed as a desktop application for reflective logging and analysis, not as a mobile or web-based service.

Distribution

ForeThought is not currently available for public download and is not listed on the Mac App Store.

There is no subscription, account system, or hosted service associated with the application.

Future availability

Decisions around wider distribution will be made once the feature set, workflows, and long-term direction have fully settled.

Any future availability will prioritise the same principles that guide the current build: clarity, simplicity, and control over data.

ForeThought is built as a long-term personal performance journal, not a short-term product launch.
