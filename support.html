Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

Lesson Logger stores your data in your chosen JSON file, which can be saved locally or in iCloud Drive.

Can I use multiple devices?

Yes. Lesson Logger can open the same JSON file across your devices via iCloud Drive.

Does Lesson Logger require an account?

No. Lesson Logger does not require registration or a subscription.

Can I export my data?

Yes. All data is stored in an open JSON format.

Is my data shared?

No. Your data remains under your control.
