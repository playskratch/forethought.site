Changelog

v1.9.x — Current

  • Fitness & non-golf activity logging
  • Coach PDF export
  • Ball & course management
  • Wedge matrix & gapping tables
  • Improved equipment tracking

v2.0 — Planned

  • Hole-level analysis
  • FIR miss direction (left / right)
  • GIR miss direction (left / right / short / long)
  • Up & down tracking per hole
  • Penalty and hazard tracking

Planned features reflect intent, not release dates.

ForeThought is macOS-only and designed for retrospective logging. There is no live shot capture during play. ForeThought is a personal golf performance journal built for macOS.
Designed and developed with a focus on clarity, reflection, and long-term improvement.
