Lesson Logger Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Lesson Logger is designed with privacy in mind.

Data Collection

Lesson Logger does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The app does not require user accounts, registration, subscriptions, analytics tracking, or advertising identifiers.

Data Storage

All data entered into Lesson Logger is stored locally by the user in a JSON file chosen by the user. This file may be stored locally on the device or within the user's own iCloud Drive storage.

Lesson Logger does not upload or store user data on any servers operated by the developer.

Calendar Access

If granted permission, Lesson Logger may access the device calendar solely to create calendar events requested by the user. Calendar data is never collected, transmitted, or stored by the developer.

Third-Party Services

Lesson Logger does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

Your Data

Users retain complete ownership and control of their data. Data may be exported at any time using the application's JSON file format.
