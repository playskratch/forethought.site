ForeThought

macOS golf performance journal

Built for reflection, not distraction.
Practice, rounds, equipment, fitness, and coach-ready PDFs.

Practice logging

Log practice sessions after you finish. Track clubs used, session focus, and notes without touching your phone on the range.

Equipment & wedge matrix

Manage bags, clubs, gapping tables, and wedge matrices — all tied to real sessions and outcomes.

Coach report export

Generate a clean, monochrome PDF summary designed to be shared directly with your coach.

ForeThought is macOS-only and designed for retrospective logging. There is no live shot capture during play.

ForeThought is a personal golf performance journal built for macOS.
Designed and developed with a focus on clarity, reflection, and long-term improvement.
