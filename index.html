Play Smarter.

Improve With Intent.

ForeThought is a macOS-native golf performance hub built for structured reflection. Log your rounds. Analyse your practice. Understand your trends. Export coach-ready reports with clarity.

Structured practice logging

Practice is where improvement happens. ForeThought captures it properly. Log sessions after you finish. Track focus areas, clubs used, outcomes, and patterns over time without touching your phone on the range.

Equipment clarity

Your bag tells a story. ForeThought connects it to performance. Manage clubs, gapping tables, and wedge matrices in one clean view. Tie yardages directly to real sessions and real outcomes.

Coach-ready reporting

Turn data into conversation. Generate refined, monochrome PDF reports designed for serious player–coach analysis. Season summaries, scoring trends, equipment breakdowns, and structured performance insights.

Built for reflection, not distraction

ForeThought is retrospective by design. There is no live shot capture. No on-course interruptions. No noise.

Log with intention. Review with clarity. Improve over time.

ForeThought is a macOS-only project in active development. Designed and developed with a focus on clarity, structure, and long-term performance improvement.
