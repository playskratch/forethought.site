How ForeThought works

ForeThought is intentionally different from live-shot tracking apps. It is designed for reflection, not distraction.

macOS-native by design

ForeThought is a macOS application built for post-round and post-session analysis. It is not intended to be used during play or practice.

Retrospective logging

All rounds, practice sessions, and fitness activities are logged after completion.

This approach encourages honest reflection without interrupting play, practice flow, or coaching sessions.

No live capture

ForeThought does not perform live shot capture, GPS tracking, or in-play interaction.

This is a deliberate design decision to keep focus on learning and review rather than real-time data collection.

Lessons handled separately

In-the-moment lesson logging is handled by the Lesson Logger Companion app.

Lessons are later imported into ForeThought, where they are reviewed and analysed alongside all other activity.

Local-first data

Data is stored locally and can be synced via iCloud Drive.

There is no mandatory account system, cloud service, or subscription.

ForeThought is macOS-only and designed for retrospective logging. There is no live shot capture during play. ForeThought is a personal golf performance journal built for macOS.
Designed and developed with a focus on clarity, reflection, and long-term improvement.
